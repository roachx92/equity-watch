## Thesis context (one-paragraph)
LPKF is the pure-play, IP-protected leader in LIDE (Laser-Induced Deep Etching) glass-substrate structuring equipment — a picks-and-shovels play on glass-core substrates for AI advanced packaging. The core business is loss-making and cyclical; the equity is priced as an option on the glass ramp (~2027 volume, customer-gated). It ran ~3x in 2026 then pulled back; every sell-side target sits below spot.

## Edge (variant view — what the crowd may have wrong)
The market has bid LPKF up ~3x on glass/LIDE optionality, yet every analyst target sits **below** spot — so consensus already says the option premium is too rich. The honest variant view leans bearish and is about timing/credibility: the crowd is pricing the glass ramp as near-certain, but it is **customer-gated and has already slipped once** (LPKF missed its own 30 Jun 2026 first-series-order deadline). The variant claim is that the timing/credibility risk is underpriced relative to the elevated option premium. (The bullish counter-edge: LIDE IP is genuinely defended — survived EPO opposition, Korea grant — and is the de-facto standard ex-Korea, so the first HVM order is "when not if." Track which way the evidence breaks.)

## Tripwires (pre-committed exit / re-underwrite triggers)
1. **A SECOND missed/slipped LIDE first-series-order date** (after the 30 Jun 2026 miss) — credibility gone, option premium deflates.
2. **A rival TGV method wins a major design** (e.g., Philoptics' variable-hole single-pass claim, or an anchor customer's in-house process) — erodes LIDE-as-standard.

4. **Glass volume-production timeline slips past 2027** (customer-gated) — pushes the whole thesis further out, deflating the premium.
