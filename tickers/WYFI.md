# WYFI — WhiteFiber, Inc. (NASDAQ: WYFI)

**Canonical deep-dive:** `WhiteFiber_WYFI_DeepDive.docx` (local). This file holds only the monitoring state.

## Thesis context (one-paragraph)
WhiteFiber is a sub-scale neocloud (GPU cloud + colocation) carved out of Bit Digital (which owns ~70%), on a thin ~11-12M-share float with very high short interest. The whole thesis reduces to one contract: the ~$865M/10-yr Nscale colocation deal is ~94% of the colo backlog. NC-1 (Madison, NC) is a genuinely powered site; the build is bridge-financed pending permanent financing. It is a levered, single-counterparty conversion story, not a diversified compounder.

## Edge (variant view — what the crowd may have wrong)
The Street lumps WYFI with money-losing neoclouds, and the July 2026 "Meta Compute" headline hit it by association (CoreWeave/Nebius fell 10-12%). The variant view: WYFI's direct customer is **Nscale, not Meta** — and Nscale just raised a $2B Series C ($14.6B post) plus a $900M revolver, so its ability to pay is stronger than the sympathy selloff implies; NC-1 is a real, permitted, powered site (power is the true bottleneck). If NC-1 financing closes and Nscale bills on schedule, the ~$921M backlog converts and WYFI re-rates largely independent of neocloud sentiment. (Cuts both ways: if you think the crowd *under*-appreciates the single-counterparty risk, the edge is bearish — either way, watch Nscale + NC-1, not the neocloud tape.)

## Tripwires (pre-committed exit / re-underwrite triggers)
1. **NC-1 permanent financing fails to close, or closes on punitive/heavily-dilutive terms** — the build stays stranded on the 9.5% related-party bridge.
2. **Nscale shows credit stress, renegotiates, or delays the colocation take** — since Nscale is ~94% of colo backlog, this breaks the core thesis (the Pre-Mortem).
3. **Q2/Q3'26 prints show the Nscale phases NOT billing on schedule** — backlog isn't converting to recognized revenue.
4. **Bit Digital signals intent to sell (or the 2026 no-sell pledge lapses and supply hits the thin float)** — dilution/overhang shock.
